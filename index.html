Memory is a living thing, evolving and changing with time. And while scent – the most evocative of our senses – is literally ephemeral, its effects are more than fleeting.

Science confirms what the history books have long shared: your sense of memory and scent are inextricably linked, with olfactory experiences holding the most direct felt connection to your memory. A moment on the nose can create, through the evolutionary wiring of your body’s limbic system, a heartfelt impression to last a lifetime. And so, for those blessed enough to experience an event for the ages, photography is simply not enough.

Drawing inspiration from the natural world – rare flora, herbs, leaves and resins of historical and evocative legacy – our award-winning aromatherapist and perfumer will work with you in advance of your event to introduce you to the world of natural essences.

A journey into the sensorially romantic to craft a unique aromatic portrait of your love. Think of it as a honeymoon before your wedding, a moment to reflect on your love, and the opportunity to create a living memory to the life of your personal cermony.




Whether for your nuptials, an anniversary, a holiday party, or the most exclusive fête of the season, our fully bespoke services will deliver a scent experience personally crafted and guaranteed by our certified aromatherapist and perfumer, Juan Felipe Rendon.

As the founder of the award-winning scent & wellness boutique shocks of love, Juan Felipe favors an embodied approach to aromatherapy and perfumary, offering sensory rituals
for clients all over the world.
